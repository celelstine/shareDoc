The payroll for the month
17/01/2017
Every child wants to say something like “good bye, mum”, but some cannot. A lot of elements limit a child’s speech such as emptiness, social phobia and inability to produce suitable sound. Stammering is the inability to produce a steady flow of sound; it has no pure cause and no perfect solution.
